LOGO PLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS
============================

1. Save your Cairde Designs logo (the gold "CD" art deco image) as:
   cairde-logo.png

2. Place it in this directory:
   /assets/images/cairde-logo.png

3. Recommended specifications:
   - Format: PNG with transparency
   - Size: 500x500px (will scale automatically)
   - File size: Keep under 200KB for fast loading

The header will automatically display your logo once uploaded.
If no logo is present, it will show a stylish text fallback.
